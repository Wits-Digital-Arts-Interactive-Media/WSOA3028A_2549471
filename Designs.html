Process to incorporate IxD into Billy Jean

As I plan out the process of implementing Interaction Design (IxD) for Billy Jeans, my fictional clothing brand, I'm reflecting on how my original wireframe ideas align with this process. Here's how I'm approaching it:

Research and Analysis

I begin by conducting research to understand the needs, preferences, and behaviors of my target audience, which primarily consists of Gen Z to millennials interested in 90s to early 2000s style baggy jeans.

Ideation and Conceptualization

Armed with insights from my research, I brainstorm ideas and concepts for the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design of the Billy Jeans website.

Prototyping and Iteration

Using tools like Adobe XD or Figma, I create interactive prototypes based on my wireframe designs.

Implementation and Testing

With a finalized design in hand, I move forward with implementing the IxD principles into the development of the Billy Jeans website.

Launch and Evaluation

After completing the development process, I launch the Billy Jeans website to the public, monitoring its performance and gathering feedback from users.
